Getting Started

How does Spare Parts Suppliers work?

Spare Parts Suppliers connects automotive parts buyers with verified suppliers worldwide. Simply search for the part you need, browse available suppliers, and connect directly to get quotes.

We don't handle transactions - we facilitate connections between buyers and suppliers, making it easier to find exactly what you need at competitive prices.

Do I need an account to search for parts?

No, you can search for parts and view supplier information without creating an account. However, to contact suppliers or request quotes, you'll need to create a free account.

Creating an account takes less than 2 minutes and gives you access to all platform features.

Is the platform free to use?

Yes, buyers can use the platform completely free. There are no fees for searching, browsing, or contacting suppliers.

Only suppliers pay subscription fees to list their parts and receive priority notifications about quote requests.

How do I create an account?

Click "Get Started" in the top navigation, then choose your account type (Buyer or Supplier). You can sign up using:

  • Email and password
  • Google account
  • Facebook account
  • Apple ID

For Suppliers

How do I become a supplier on the platform?

To become a supplier, create an account and select "Supplier" during registration. You'll need to provide:

  • Business registration documents
  • Tax identification number
  • Business address and contact information
  • Bank account details (for premium plans)

Our verification team will review your application within 24-48 hours.

What's the difference between free and premium supplier accounts?

Free Account:

  • 5 active part listings
  • 24-hour notification delay
  • Basic support via FAQ

Premium Account:

  • Up to 200+ active listings (depending on plan)
  • Instant quote notifications
  • Priority placement in search results
  • Verified supplier badges
  • Advanced analytics and reporting
  • Priority customer support
How do I add parts to my inventory?

After logging into your supplier dashboard, click "Add Parts" and provide:

  • Part number (OEM or aftermarket)
  • Part description and specifications
  • Compatible vehicle models
  • Part category (engine, brake, electrical, etc.)
  • Part images (optional but recommended)
  • Availability and stock levels
How quickly will I receive quote requests?

Premium suppliers receive instant email notifications when buyers search for their parts. Free suppliers receive notifications with a 24-hour delay.

Response time is crucial - suppliers who respond within 2 hours have 5x higher conversion rates.

For Buyers

How do I search for parts effectively?

You can search using several methods:

  • Part Number: Most accurate - use OEM or aftermarket numbers
  • Vehicle Info: Make, model, year (e.g., "BMW 5 Series 2015")
  • Part Description: General terms (e.g., "brake pads", "headlight")
  • Advanced Filters: Brand, category, supplier location, year range
Are all suppliers verified?

All suppliers undergo a verification process before joining the platform. However, there are different levels of verification:

  • Basic Verified: Identity and business registration confirmed
  • Premium Verified: Additional financial and reference checks
  • Gold/Diamond: Long-term track record with excellent ratings

Look for verification badges and ratings when choosing suppliers.

How do I request quotes from suppliers?

To request quotes:

  • Search for your part and click "Request Quote"
  • Login or create a free account if prompted
  • Provide details: quantity needed, delivery location, timeline
  • Click "Send Quote Requests" to notify all relevant suppliers

Premium suppliers typically respond within 2 hours, others within 24 hours.

Do I pay through the platform?

No, we don't handle payments or transactions. Spare Parts Suppliers is a connection platform that helps you find suppliers.

All negotiations, payments, and shipping arrangements are made directly between you and the supplier. This keeps costs low and gives you maximum flexibility.

Billing & Plans

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit Cards: Visa, MasterCard, American Express
  • PayPal
  • Bank Transfer (for Enterprise plans)
  • Apple Pay and Google Pay

All payments are processed securely through Stripe with 256-bit SSL encryption.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account dashboard. Your plan will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

If you cancel within 30 days, you're eligible for a full refund under our money-back guarantee.

Do you offer refunds?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact our support team for a full refund.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to your original payment method.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time from your account dashboard. Changes take effect immediately:

  • Upgrades: You pay the prorated difference and get instant access to new features
  • Downgrades: Account credit is applied to future billing cycles
