Company Description

Global Auto Parts Ltd is a leading supplier of genuine and aftermarket automotive parts for European vehicles. Founded in 1987 in Hamburg, Germany, we have built a reputation for quality, reliability, and exceptional customer service. Our extensive inventory includes parts for BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, Volkswagen, and other premium European brands.

Specializations

  • BMW Original Equipment & Aftermarket Parts
  • Mercedes-Benz Genuine Components
  • Audi Performance & Standard Parts
  • Volkswagen Complete Range
  • Engine, Transmission & Suspension Systems

Quality Assurance

All parts undergo rigorous quality control checks. We maintain ISO 9001:2015 certification and work directly with OEM manufacturers to ensure authenticity and reliability.
